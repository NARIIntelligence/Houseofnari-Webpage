Incredible India - Complete Guide

Discover all states, union territories, and islands of our great nation

States (28)
Andhra Pradesh

Capital: Amaravati. Known for Tirupati temple, spicy cuisine, and Kuchipudi dance.

Arunachal Pradesh

Capital: Itanagar. "Land of Dawn-Lit Mountains" with diverse tribal culture.

Assam

Capital: Dispur. Famous for tea gardens, silk, and Kaziranga National Park.

Bihar

Capital: Patna. Birthplace of Buddhism and Jainism, ancient Nalanda University.

Chhattisgarh

Capital: Raipur. Rich in minerals, tribal culture, and dense forests.

Goa

Capital: Panaji. Beautiful beaches, Portuguese heritage, and vibrant nightlife.

Gujarat

Capital: Gandhinagar. Business hub, birthplace of Mahatma Gandhi, Rann of Kutch.

Haryana

Capital: Chandigarh (shared). Agricultural state, wrestling tradition, Kurukshetra.

Himachal Pradesh

Capital: Shimla. Hill stations, apple orchards, and Buddhist monasteries.

Jharkhand

Capital: Ranchi. Mineral-rich state, tribal festivals, and Jain pilgrimage sites.

Karnataka

Capital: Bangalore. IT hub, Mysore Palace, classical music, and coffee plantations.

Kerala

Capital: Thiruvananthapuram. "God's Own Country" with backwaters and Ayurveda.

Madhya Pradesh

Capital: Bhopal. Heart of India, Khajuraho temples, and wildlife sanctuaries.

Maharashtra

Capital: Mumbai. Financial capital, Bollywood, and Maratha heritage.

Manipur

Capital: Imphal. "Jewel of India" with classical dance and polo origins.

Meghalaya

Capital: Shillong. "Abode of Clouds" with living root bridges and matriarchal society.

Mizoram

Capital: Aizawl. Mountainous state with unique culture and bamboo forests.

Nagaland

Capital: Kohima. Land of festivals, tribal heritage, and hornbill birds.

Odisha

Capital: Bhubaneswar. Jagannath Temple, Sun Temple Konark, and Odissi dance.

Punjab

Capital: Chandigarh (shared). Land of five rivers, Golden Temple, and Bhangra.

Rajasthan

Capital: Jaipur. Desert state with magnificent forts, palaces, and folk culture.

Sikkim

Capital: Gangtok. Himalayan state with Buddhist monasteries and organic farming.

Tamil Nadu

Capital: Chennai. Ancient temples, classical arts, and Dravidian architecture.

Telangana

Capital: Hyderabad. IT hub, Charminar, and famous biryani cuisine.

Tripura

Capital: Agartala. Rich in handicrafts, bamboo products, and tribal culture.

Uttar Pradesh

Capital: Lucknow. Most populous state, Taj Mahal, and holy cities like Varanasi.

Uttarakhand

Capital: Dehradun. "Land of Gods" with Char Dham pilgrimage and hill stations.

West Bengal

Capital: Kolkata. Cultural capital, Durga Puja, and Bengali literature.

Union Territories (8)
Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Capital: Port Blair. Tropical paradise with pristine beaches and cellular jail history.

Chandigarh

Capital: Chandigarh. Planned city, shared capital of Punjab and Haryana.

Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu

Capital: Daman. Portuguese heritage, beaches, and tribal culture.

Delhi (NCT)

Capital: New Delhi. National capital with Red Fort, India Gate, and diverse culture.

Jammu and Kashmir

Summer Capital: Srinagar, Winter Capital: Jammu. "Paradise on Earth" with beautiful valleys.

Ladakh

Capital: Leh. High-altitude desert, Buddhist monasteries, and adventure tourism.

Lakshadweep

Capital: Kavaratti. Coral islands in Arabian Sea with pristine lagoons.

Puducherry

Capital: Puducherry. French colonial heritage and spiritual center Auroville.

Indian Ocean Territories
Andaman Islands

Major islands: North Andaman, Middle Andaman, South Andaman. Rich marine biodiversity.

Nicobar Islands

Major islands: Car Nicobar, Great Nicobar. Indigenous tribes and coconut plantations.

Lakshadweep Islands

10 inhabited islands including Kavaratti, Agatti, Minicoy. Coral reef paradise.

Katchal Island

Part of Nicobar group. Tsunami-affected area now rebuilt with modern infrastructure.

Ross Island

Former British administrative center, now a tourist destination with ruins.

Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep)

Popular tourist destination with beautiful beaches and coral reefs.
